# AI Logo & Icon Generation Prompt for SASTASPACE

## Project Overview
**SASTASPACE** is a revolutionary portfolio hosting platform that democratizes professional portfolio creation. The name combines "Sasta" (affordable/accessible in Hindi) with "Space" to represent affordable digital space for everyone to showcase their work.

## Brand Identity Requirements

### Company Name & Meaning
- **SASTASPACE** (pronounced "Sasta Space")
- **Core Concept**: "Affordable Space for Everyone's Portfolio"
- **Mission**: Democratizing portfolio hosting - making professional portfolios accessible to all
- **Value Proposition**: Premium portfolio hosting at accessible prices

### Target Audience
- **Students and recent graduates** building their first portfolio
- **Freelancers and gig workers** needing affordable portfolio hosting
- **Career changers** transitioning to new fields
- **Small business owners** showcasing their work
- **International professionals** from diverse backgrounds
- **Anyone** who wants a professional portfolio without breaking the bank

### Brand Personality
- **Inclusive and welcoming** - "Portfolio hosting for everyone"
- **Modern and cutting-edge** - Latest technology and design trends
- **Accessible and affordable** - Premium quality without premium prices
- **Empowering and supportive** - Helping people succeed
- **Global and diverse** - Welcoming users from all backgrounds

## Design Requirements

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Modern Blue (#3B82F6) - Trust, technology, accessibility
- **Secondary**: Growth Green (#10B981) - Success, opportunity, affordability
- **Accent**: Energetic Orange (#F59E0B) - Creativity, innovation, warmth
- **Supporting**: Soft Purple (#8B5CF6) - Innovation, inclusivity
- **Neutral**: Clean whites and modern grays

### Style Guidelines
- **Modern and inclusive design language**
- **Clean, geometric shapes with soft edges**
- **Scalable and responsive design**
- **Professional yet approachable**
- **Works across all cultures and backgrounds**

## Additional Notes
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Ensure the design works across different cultures and backgrounds
- **Accessibility**: Design should be clear and recognizable for users with different abilities
- **Global Appeal**: Consider how the design translates across international markets
- **Modern Feel**: Should feel cutting-edge and contemporary
- **Inclusive Language**: Avoid any elements that might exclude or alienate users
- **Scalability**: Must work well at all sizes and contexts
- **Memorability**: Should be distinctive and memorable while being approachable

## Design Philosophy
The logo should embody the belief that **everyone deserves access to professional portfolio hosting** - regardless of their background, location, or budget. It should feel modern, inclusive, and empowering while maintaining professional credibility.
