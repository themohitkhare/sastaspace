Questions

Things people ask.

Do I really have to photograph every piece I own?

For best results, yes — but you don't need to do it all at once. Most beta users catalogue in 20-minute batches over a week. Threadly also supports bulk-upload from a closet shelf or rack photo, where it auto-segments individual items.

Where do shopping suggestions come from?

We integrate with a curated set of retailers and small brands. Suggestions are ranked purely by how well an item fits your existing wardrobe — we don't take placement fees or prioritize sponsored items.

What about privacy? My closet is personal.

Photos are processed and stored encrypted. Your wardrobe is never used to train models for other users. You can export or delete everything at any time. Local-first sync is on the roadmap for power users.

How much will it cost?

Free to catalogue and get daily outfits for up to 100 items. Premium ($9/mo) unlocks unlimited items, calendar integration, and shopping intelligence. Beta users keep premium free for life.

When does it launch?

Private beta opens in waves through Spring 2026. Waitlist signups get early access in the order they joined, with a referral boost.
